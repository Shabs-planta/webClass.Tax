1. padding - is the distance between an element and the surrounding
       types of padding
- padding top
- padding buttom
- padding left
- padding right

padding: 2px; (top, bottom, left and right)
padding: 2px 5px; (top&bottom and left&right)

padding: 2px 3px 5px 1px; (top, right, bottom, left)

padding-top: 2px;
padding-bottom: 5px;
padding-left: 3px;
padding-right: 1px;


2. marging - is the distance between two elements 
       types of marging
- marging top
- marging buttom
- marging left
- marging right

marging: 2px; (top, bottom, left and right)
marging: 2px 5px; (top&bottom and left&right)

marging: 2px 3px 5px 1px; (top, right, bottom, left)

marging-top: 2px;
marging-buttom: 5px;
